About me

I'm Dinidu Theekshana, the CEO of ArtDex Studio, with 4 years of professional experience as a Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, Digital Artist, and 3D Designer. My expertise includes branding and identity design, user-centric UI/UX design, digital illustrations, motion graphics, and 3D modeling for diverse projects. I am passionate about creating visually compelling designs that captivate audiences and deliver effective user experiences.

Throughout my career, I have worked with clients across various industries, ranging from startups to established brands, ensuring that each design solution is uniquely tailored to meet their objectives. I believe in a collaborative approach, where understanding the client's vision is key to transforming ideas into impactful visual narratives.

At ArtDex Studio, I lead a talented team committed to pushing creative boundaries and delivering high-quality design solutions that effectively communicate brand stories. I am constantly exploring new tools and trends in the design world to stay ahead of the curve and ensure our work remains innovative and relevant.
